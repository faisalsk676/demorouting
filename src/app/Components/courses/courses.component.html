Course One

LEVEL-3 is a course designed to reduce examination burden which a student usually faces while opting for A-Levels and F.A. The course has a specially designed structure in which a student has to prepare the assignments provided by PEARSON and appear in an examination conducted by the Center (CSPS). LEVEL-3 is a course aimed at students who are constantly failing or finding A-Levels and F.A difficult. LEVEL-3 gives student an easy pathway towards their Higher Education. LEVEL-3 is a UK qualification recognized by Education Board of Pakistan that means LEVEL-3 fulfills the criteria for a Bachelor’s Degree and also Higher National Diploma in Business (at CSPS).

Course Two

HND is short for Higher National Diploma.It is a work-related Business and Technology Education Council (BTEC) qualification offered by the Pearson board -one of the largest UK awarding bodies. HND provides students with hands-on coursework along with an opportunity to go to a UK university in the final year of Bachelor’s Studies to complete his/her degree.Pakistan’s Higher Education Commission (HEC) has given HND the equivalency of two years of a Bachelor’s degree.
While a Bachelor’s degree tends to focus on gaining knowledge,HND is designed to provide you with skills to utilize that knowledge in a particular job.HND takes two years to complete and is highly valued by employers around the globe.It is provided by about 400 universities and further education colleges in the UK.
